Privacy Policy for Habitry

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Habitry is a simple, offline habit and streak tracker app developed by an independent developer.

We Do Not Collect Any Personal Data

Habitry does not collect, use, store, or share any personal information from users.

  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying information
  • No location data
  • No device information, advertising IDs, or analytics data
  • No crash reports or usage statistics
  • No internet connection is required or used
  • No third-party services, SDKs, trackers, or advertisements are included

How Your Data Is Handled

All your habits, completion dates, and streak information are stored exclusively on your device using Android's local Room database. Nothing is ever sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Permissions

The app requires no permissions beyond standard app operation. It does not access:

  • Internet
  • Location
  • Camera / Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Storage (beyond its own app data)
  • Notifications

Changes to This Policy

If the app is ever updated to include any data collection features in the future (which is not planned), this privacy policy will be updated and users will be informed through the Google Play Store listing.
