Privacy Policy for Habitry

Effective Date: February 09, 2026

Habitry is a completely offline habit and streak tracker app developed by Madiha (individual developer from Islamabad, Pakistan).

We Collect No Personal Data

Habitry does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from its users.

  • No name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics
  • No internet connection is ever used by the app
  • No third-party SDKs, trackers, ads, or services are included

Data Storage

All your habits, completion dates, and streak information are stored **locally on your device** only, using Android's built-in Room database. Nothing is ever sent to any server, cloud, or third party.

Permissions

The app requires **zero dangerous permissions**. It does not access camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage (beyond its own app data), internet, or notifications.

Changes to This Policy

If the app is ever updated in the future to collect any data (which is extremely unlikely), this policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be informed via the Google Play Store listing.
